# De-LLM Examples Reference

Extended examples from Wikipedia's "Signs of AI Writing" to help identify and fix LLM tells.

## Puffery and Significance Claims

### Before → After

**Undue emphasis on significance:**
> Kumba has long been an important center for trade and agriculture. [...] The establishment of road networks connecting Kumba to other parts of the Southwest Region helped solidify its role as a regional hub.

→ Kumba is a trade and agricultural center. Road networks connect it to Mamfe and Buea in the Southwest Region.

**Etymology with unnecessary analysis:**
> This etymology highlights the enduring legacy of the community's resistance and the transformative power of unity in shaping its identity.

→ Remove entirely, or: The name reflects the community's history.

**Biology with ecosystem puffery:**
> It plays a role in the ecosystem and contributes to Hawaii's rich cultural heritage. [...] Preserving this endemic species is vital not only for ecological diversity but also for sustaining the cultural traditions connected to Hawaii's native flora.

→ It is endemic to Hawaii. [Add specific ecological facts if sourced]

## Superficial Analyses

### Before → After

**Trailing -ing phrases:**
> The civil rights movement emerged as a powerful continuation of this struggle, emphasizing the importance of solidarity and collective action in the fight for justice.

→ The civil rights movement continued this struggle through solidarity and collective action.

**Temple description overdone:**
> The temple's color palette of blue, green, and gold resonates with the region's natural beauty, symbolizing Texas bluebonnets, the Gulf of Mexico, and the diverse Texan landscapes.

→ The temple's blue, green, and gold colors reference Texas bluebonnets and the Gulf of Mexico.

## Promotional Language

### Before → After

**Tourism description:**
> Nestled within the breathtaking region of Gonder in Ethiopia, Alamata Raya Kobo stands as a vibrant town with a rich cultural heritage and a significant place within the Amhara region.

→ Alamata Raya Kobo is a town in the Gonder region of Ethiopia's Amhara Region.

**Product description:**
> The SOLLEI's exterior design communicates a powerful emotional presence, staying true to Cadillac's signature bold proportions.

→ The SOLLEI has Cadillac's characteristic large proportions.

## Copula Avoidance

### Before → After

> Gallery 825 on La Cienega Boulevard serves as LAAA's exhibition space for contemporary art. The gallery features four separate spaces.

→ Gallery 825 on La Cienega Boulevard is LAAA's exhibition space for contemporary art. The gallery has four separate spaces.

> Harian Metro holds the distinction of being the first and oldest Malay-language tabloid.

→ Harian Metro is the first and oldest Malay-language tabloid.

## Negative Parallelisms

### Before → After

> It's not just about the beat riding under the vocals; it's part of the aggression and atmosphere.

→ The beat contributes to the track's aggression and atmosphere.

> Not only a work of self-representation, but a visual document of her obsessions.

→ A self-portrait that documents her obsessions.

## Rule of Three

### Before → After

> The event features keynote sessions, panel discussions, and networking opportunities.

→ The event features keynote sessions and panel discussions.

> global SEO professionals, marketing experts, and growth hackers

→ SEO and marketing professionals

## Vague Attributions

### Before → After

> His compositions have been described as exploring conceptual themes.

→ [Either cite who said this, or remove]

> Due to its unique characteristics, the Haolai River is of interest to researchers and conservationists.

→ [Remove unless you can cite specific researchers]

## Challenge/Future Patterns

### Before → After

> Despite its industrial and residential prosperity, Korattur faces challenges typical of urban areas. [...] With its strategic location and ongoing initiatives, Korattur continues to thrive.

→ Korattur faces urban challenges including [specific issues]. [Remove vague positive conclusion]

> Despite their promising applications, pyroelectric materials face several challenges that must be addressed for broader adoption.

→ Pyroelectric materials have limitations including [specific limitations].

## AI Vocabulary Density

High-density example (multiple tells in one passage):

> Additionally, Somali merchants played a pivotal role in the global coffee trade, being one of the first to export coffee beans. [...] An enduring testament to the influence of Italian colonial rule in Somalia is the widespread adoption of pasta and lasagne in the local culinary landscape, showcasing how these dishes have integrated into the traditional diet.

→ Somali merchants were among the first to export coffee beans. Italian colonial rule introduced pasta and lasagne, now common in Somali cuisine.

## Meta-Commentary

### Before → After

> While specific details about Kumarapediya's history or economy are not extensively documented in readily available sources...

→ [Remove and only state what you can verify]

> As of my last knowledge update in January 2022, I don't have specific information about...

→ [Remove entirely - this is an AI artifact]

## Formatting Issues

### Title Case
> Key Statistics of Indian Biobanking (2024-2025)

→ Key statistics of Indian biobanking (2024–2025)

### Inline-Header Lists
> * **SEO (Search Engine Optimization):** Traditional methods for improving visibility...
> * **AEO (Answer Engine Optimization):** Techniques focused on optimizing content...

→ Convert to prose or use simpler bullet structure without bold headers.

### Em Dash Overuse
> you're right about one thing — we do seem to have different interpretations — and that's okay — but let me explain.

→ You're right that we have different interpretations. Let me explain.
